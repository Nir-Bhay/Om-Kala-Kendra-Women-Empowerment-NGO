About Om Kala Kendra

Om Kala Kendra is a Chhindwara-based NGO dedicated to empowering individuals through skill development and vocational training. With programs tailored to tailoring, fashion design, and beauty courses, we have transformed countless lives, offering sustainable career opportunities and personal growth.

Our mission is to nurture talent, uplift communities, and create a future where every individual has the skills to thrive and succeed.
